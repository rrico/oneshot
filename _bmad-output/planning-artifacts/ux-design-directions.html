oneshot — design direction showcase

Eight play-direction explorations plus selected D1+D6 hybrid and four authoring mocks (Spotify / Apple Music–inspired). Open on desktop or narrow the window.

Evaluate: hierarchy, CTA clarity, attempt UI legibility, and fit with “simple, modern, clean.” Hover states omitted—this is layout and weight only.

Selected · D1 + D6

Hybrid — D1 landing & chrome + D6 playback progress

Landing stays Direction 1 (minimal dark, neutral CTA). Play keeps D1 dots + guess + skip, a Heardle-style snippet progress bar (cyan→violet fill from D6) with elapsed / clip end time, and a missed-guesses list—every wrong autocomplete pick stays visible for the round (title + artist, muted—like classic Heardle). Attempt state stays on the six dots. Clip length follows the PRD ladder (1s / 2s / 4s / …).
